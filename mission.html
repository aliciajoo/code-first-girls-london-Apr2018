Our mission

Give women the opportunity to advance in male-dominated industries, by providing a platform to discover information unique to their experience of the working world.



Meet the team

Harriet

Harriet currently works in Market Risk at Goldman Sachs. Having recently graduated with a degree in Mathematics, she is passionate about encouraging young women and girls to pursue STEM subjects.





Alicia

Alicia is an Associate in Internal Audit at Goldman Sachs. Prior to this, she was a Manager at Deloitte having worked both in London and in New York. She is passionate about connecting young female working professionals in a global network that empowers each other.

Anastasiia has joined Risk Graduate Scheme at Lloyds Banking Group after graduating from Cass Business School with BSc Investment and Financial Risk Management. The degree not only allowed Anastasiia to carry out quantitative analyses, but also provided her with necessary skills to do qualitative analyses.
